Mental Health Awareness

The purpose of this project is to raise awareness about mental health on for young people, especially on college campuses. The medium posts, podcast, and video tackle different issues and address stories that are centered around mental health.

Medium Posts

The creative directive of the medium posts is to tell stories that are central to the mental health dialogue. "Mental Illness Stigma Is Making Us Sicker" tackles the growing concerns of stigma in society. "Carrie's Candid Confessions" addresses the story of famed actress Carrie Fisher and her struggles with mental illness alongside her lifelong committment to mental health advocacy. "America's Broken Mental Health System" touches on the political and civil shortcomings that plague America's mental health system--an issue critical to the millenial generation. "The Story Of One Girl's Mental Health Journey" tells an intimate story of a college student who was diagnosed with chronic depression. Visit my medium profile to read more.
